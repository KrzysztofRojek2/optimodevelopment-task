Preparation:
-Defining the budget and schedule
-Choosing the style and color scheme
-Purchasing necessary materials and tools
-Moving out furniture and securing the floor

Preliminary work:
-Removing old paint
-Repairing holes in the walls and ceiling
-Sanding the surfaces

Painting and finishing:
-Painting the ceiling and walls
-Wallpapering
-Installing baseboards

Flooring:
-Assessing the condition of the floor
-Installing panels, tiles, or carpeting

Installation and arrangement:
-Installing lighting and outlets
-Placing furniture and decorations
-Cleaning up and final touch-ups
